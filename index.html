About me

Iam Joanne Rowling,pen name J.K. Rowling, a British author, screenwriter, producer and philanthropist. I earned my BA in French and Classics at the the University of Exeter. I graduated from Exeter in 1988. Till now, I have written several books which includes Harry Potter series, which has won multiple awards and sold more than 500millon copies; "Casual Vacancy", "Fantastic Beasts",etc. Popular Harry Potter film series is also made on the basis of my books.I also worked as a producer on the final two-part instalment of the movie, Deathly Hallows. I also gave a voice cameo in "The Regina Monologues" .I also write crime fiction under the pen name Robert Galbraith. I am also a member of both English PEN and Scottish PEN. I was one of 50 authors to contribute to First Editions, Second Thoughts, a charity auction for English PEN.

About me 2

Ihave received honorary degrees from St Andrews University, the University of Edinburgh, Edinburgh Napier University, the University of Exeter (which I attended), the University of Aberdeen, and Harvard University, where I spoke at the 2008 commencement ceremony.In 2009,I was made a Chevalier de la Légion d'honneur by French President Nicolas Sarkozy. In 2002, I became an Honorary Fellow of the Royal Society of Edinburgh (HonFRSE) as well a Fellow of the Royal Society of Literature (FRSL). I was furthermore recognized as Fellow of the Royal College of Physicians of Edinburgh (FRCPE) in 2011 for services to Literature and Philanthropy . As a philanthropist, In 2000, I established the Volant Charitable Trust, which uses its annual budget of £5.1 million to combat poverty and social inequality. The fund also gives to organisations that aid children, one-parent families, and multiple sclerosis research. I am also a supporter of the Shannon Trust, which runs the Toe by Toe Reading Plan and the Shannon Reading Plan in prisons across Britain, helping and giving tutoring to prisoners who cannot read.
